  • The goal of this minigame is for you to practice what you learned the previous module (1. Flashcards (easy)).
  • For each of the 6 types of pitches, you will be asked to select their speed (slow, medium, or fast) and demonstrate their movement.
  • For each type of pitch perform the following:
    1. To select speed: press the circle that represents the speed of that type of pitch.
    2. To demonstrate movement: drag and drop the ball in the correct direction. If the pitch has no movement, you do not need to perform any action for this step.
  • After completing both steps, press the Submit button or press the Enter/Return key on your keyboard.
  • After submission you will be told whether you are correct.
  • In order to move on to the next pitch, you must get both the speed and movement correct.
  • Use the Next Pitch button to go to attempt the next type of pitch
  • Use the Restart Pitch button to clear your speed and movement demonstration for the current type of pitch.
  • Use the Restart Minigame button to restart the entire minigame (i.e. start with the first type of pitch).
  • Note: Each time the minigame is started/restarted, the order in which each type of pitch appears will be randomized.
